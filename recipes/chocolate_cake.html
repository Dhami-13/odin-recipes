Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate cake is made with chocolate. It can also include other ingredients. These include fudge, vanilla creme, and other sweeteners. The history of chocolate cake goes back to the 17th century, when cocoa powder from the Americas was added to traditional cake recipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer.
  5. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin.
  6. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  7. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack.
  8. Enjoy!
